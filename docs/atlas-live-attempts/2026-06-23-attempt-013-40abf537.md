## Key Takeaways

1. **Tasks 10-12 fixes are holding**: Zero length stops across both 012 and 013 (8 attempts). Duplicate Limitations deduplication working. Truncation either 0% or well below 10%.
2. **Rejection ratio is the hardest fix**: Task 11 (reduce rejection) shows no improvement in live tests. Both domains exceed 70% rejection. May need complementary approach — broader initial search, more permissive quality thresholds, or different source curation strategy.
3. **Consumer product queries are harder**: The robot vacuum prompt produced a fallback report. Atlas performs better on technical/enterprise queries (embedding models). This may be inherent to the evidence quality available for consumer products vs. technical topics.
4. **Non-deterministic structural issues exist**: The broken table from 012 did not reproduce here, confirming it's a model output quality fluke, not a code regression.
